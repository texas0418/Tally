Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Tally is built so your information stays with you. We designed it to work without an account, without a server, and without collecting your data.

What we collect

Nothing. Tally has no sign-up and no account. Your bills, the people you add, items, tips, and totals are stored only in the Tally app on your device. We never see them.

Receipt scanning

When you scan a receipt, the text is recognized entirely on your iPhone using Apple's on-device machine learning (Google ML Kit). The photo is processed locally and is never uploaded to us or anyone else, and we do not store it.

Purchases

Tally Pro is a one-time in-app purchase handled by Apple. To unlock and restore your purchase, we use RevenueCat, which receives anonymized purchase information — a random app-user identifier and your App Store transaction. It never receives your name, email, or the contents of your bills.

Analytics and tracking

None. Tally contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Backups

Any backup you export is a plain file that you choose where to save. It stays under your control; we never receive it.

Children

Tally is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the date above.
