How do I split one item between people?

Tap the item, then tap everyone who shared it. Tally divides it evenly among them, to the cent, and shows "Split N ways."

How does receipt scanning work?

Tap "Scan receipt" and point your camera at the bill. Text is read on your device — nothing is uploaded. Then check the items and assign them. The first few scans are free; Tally Pro unlocks unlimited scanning.

How are tip and tax split?

By each person's share of the bill — proportional, never a flat even split — so nobody overpays. The per-person totals always add up to the exact grand total.

A "2X" item should go to different people

Open the item and tap "Split into N separate items." Each unit becomes its own line you can assign individually.

How do I restore my Tally Pro purchase?

Settings → Tally Pro → "Restore purchases." Your unlock is tied to your Apple ID and restores on any device.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything stays on your phone — no account, no cloud, works offline. See our Privacy Policy.
